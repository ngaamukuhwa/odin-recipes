Garlic Chicken

Description

A classic step-by-step recipe for delicious Garlic Chicken made entirely from scratch! A quick and easy recipe that doesn't compromise on packing a punch of mouth-watering flavours.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon seasoning salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and sprinkle with garlic powder, seasoning salt, and onion powder.
  3. Saut√© until chicken is cooked through and juices run clear, 10 to 15 minutes on each side.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
